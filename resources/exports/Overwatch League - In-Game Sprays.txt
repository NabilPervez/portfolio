Tagging For The Teams
Blizzard approached us for the opportunity to be the first agency of record to work directly with studio to put assets in Overwatch.

We jumped at the opportunity and reached out to work with the perfect illustrator, Evan EckartWe immediately began working with every Overwatch team to brainstorm, revise, and eventually create in-game sprays the fans could be proud of.
