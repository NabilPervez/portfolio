TITLE: Immortal: Gates Of Pyre - Video Producing
TYPE: portfolio
====================

Effective Video Production
With the marketing for Immortal: Gates of Pyre ramping up, the AOE team was looking effectively kill two birds with one stone.

 	Build up the Youtube channel with new content
 	Generate additional content for social media without additional time commitments

I lead the project with these goals in mind to build Youtube content made of multiple smaller segments to be clipped from the original video and given to the social media team as ammo.

This involved a lot of close work with the Sunspear Games, graphic design, motion, marketing, and video editing teams to create strong final products.
Scope

 	Video Producing
 	Project Management
 	Social Media Strategy
 	Design Consulting
