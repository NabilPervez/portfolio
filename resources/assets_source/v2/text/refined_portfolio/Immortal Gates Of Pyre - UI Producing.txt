The Challenge

The Solution
I facilitated tight collaboration between our design team and Sunspear's developers. We iterated relentlessly, balancing aesthetic desires with technical constraints. We focused on creating a "functional beauty" that enhanced gameplay without distracting from it.

The Outcome
After months of revisions and testing, we delivered a finalized UI/UX package for the HUD, menus, and game client. The result is a seamless interface that players currently use, successfully merging the game's rich visual identity with professional-grade usability.
